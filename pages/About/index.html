About

Hichame El Khalfi is VP of Engineering ( and a software engineer by :heart: ) at Converge Consumer By Deloitte.

In this role, he leads the Predict and Optimization Engineering teams that supports building, running and operating Forecasting and Optimization products at scale.

Prior to joining Deloitte in 2018, Hichame was a Senior Data Engineer at Magnetic (AI driven ad-tech company acquired by Deloitte Digital) as well as Senior Software Engineer roles across multiple financial firms including Sociéte Générale CIB, Natixis and La Banque Postale.

Hichame holds a Master of Science in Distributed Systems Engineering and a Master in Business Informatics both from Université Paris-Est Créteil.
