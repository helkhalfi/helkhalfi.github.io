About Me

Oct 1, 2021 · 98 words · 1 minute read

Hichame El Khalfi is a Software Engineer (and Manager) at Hux by Deloitte Digital.

In this role, he leads the Data Engineering Team that supports building, running and scaling the organization’s AI Decisioning platform.

Hichame focuses on building and scaling reliable, automated and self-healing pipelines.

Prior to joining Deloitte Digital in 2018, Hichame worked at Magnetic (AI driven ad-tech company acquired by Deloitte Digital) as well as financial firms including Sociéte Générale CIB ,Natixis, La Banque Postale.

Hichame holds a Master of Science in Distributed Systems Engineering and a Master in Business Informatics both from Université Paris-Est Créteil.
